The article introduces the Writers' Club in Foz do Iguaçu, a community organization founded in May 2023 to support authors and creative individuals seeking professional development and personal growth through literary and artistic expression. The Consciência Café hosts this club's meetings, which occur every 15 days featuring workshops and activities, with free participation available via Instagram (@busqueaharmonia) or phone.
